FAQ & Help

Common Questions
How do I receive my payout?
▼
Payouts are sent automatically to your registered UPI ID within 90 seconds of a verified trigger. No action required from you.
What counts as an extreme rain trigger?
▼
A Red Alert issued by IMD for your zone, lasting at least 3 continuous hours. Standard Shield also covers Orange Alerts for ≥2 hours.
Can I pause my coverage?
▼
Yes, you can pause weekly auto-renewal from Settings. Your coverage remains active until the current week ends.
What if my GPS was acting up during a trigger?
▼
Our system uses 6 signals — not just GPS. If you're flagged (🟡 Soft Hold), you'll receive an SMS asking for a 1-tap confirmation. Workers with Trust Badges are auto-approved.
Is vehicle repair covered?
▼
No. GigShield covers income loss only — not vehicle repair, health, life, or accident bills. See our policy exclusions for the full list.
How is my premium calculated?
▼
Base Rate × Zone Risk Multiplier (0.8–1.6) × Earnings Exposure Factor × (1 − Safety Discount up to 20%). AI recalculates weekly based on current data.
